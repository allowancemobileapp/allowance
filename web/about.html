Welcome to Allowance

Allowance is the ultimate university campus companion app.

Our application allows students to order food from campus vendors, buy and manage tickets for campus events, and share updates, stories, and moments with their peers.

By signing in with Google, we use your basic profile information (Name and Email) to securely create your Allowance account so you can start interacting with your campus community.
